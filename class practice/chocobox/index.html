our story

YAAS CHOCOLATE with the story

In 2024, Yasmin founded YAAS Chocolate in Verona, Italy, inspired by her grandmother's secret recipes and a passion for creating chocolates that celebrated life's sweet moments. Combining rich cocoa from Ecuador, vanilla from Madagascar, and Himalayan salt, her signature YAAS Delights bar—featuring dark cocoa, caramelized orange zest, and espresso—became an instant hit. With the motto "Say Yes to Sweet Moments," YAAS quickly gained popularity, symbolizing celebration and indulgence. By 2026, it was a beloved luxury brand across Europe, praised for its unique flavors and craftsmanship. Beyond taste, YAAS stood for conscious luxury, ensuring all ingredients were ethically sourced and production sustainable. Yasmin's vision created a global brand that continues to inspire, reminding people to embrace joy and always say “Yaas!” to life's special moments.

About YAAS Chocolate

Founded in 2024 in Verona, Italy, YAAS Chocolate is a premium brand dedicated to creating extraordinary chocolate experiences that celebrate life's sweetest moments. Inspired by rich family traditions, our founder, Yasmin, infuses each creation with passion and authenticity. We believe that every bite should bring joy. Our chocolates are crafted from the finest ethically sourced ingredients, including rich Ecuadorian cocoa and aromatic Madagascar vanilla. Our signature YAAS Delights feature unique flavor combinations that elevate any occasion, from festive celebrations to everyday indulgences. Committed to sustainability, we support local farmers and promote responsible sourcing practices. With a vibrant design aesthetic and a philosophy that encourages joy and togetherness, YAAS Chocolate inspires everyone to say “Yaas!” to life's delicious moments. Join us in savoring the sweetness of chocolate and making every moment a reason to celebrate!
